What is a DuoFolio?

Just like where a portfolio describes a person's profile, DuoFolio consists of the profiles of 2 individuals combined in one website, which helps the recruiters or the viewers have a better idea of the team they are dealing with.

Do we really need it?

Be it in job approvals or going for a meeting where there's a group of unknown people or if you want to promote something online and the customers can check you out before hand whether you are legit or not is always handy of you possess a portfolio. So yes you do need a portfolio.
